| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **URL** | https://jack-clark.net/ |
| **Why useful** | Weekly roundup of the most important AI research papers with technical commentary; Jack Clark co-founded Anthropic and OpenAI; consistently high signal-to-noise ratio |
| **Difficulty** | Advanced |
| **Prerequisites** | ML fundamentals |
| **Best time to use** | Phase 10 (start when doing research) |
